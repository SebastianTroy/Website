## Description
One of my favorite cheat meals, very quick and easy to prepare, and interactive to eat.

## Ingredients
- 250g chicken
- panko breadcrumbs
- 3 tbsp dried mixed herbs
- 1 large egg
- 50g plain flour
- 50g smoked paprika
- pinch of salt
- dash of chilli flakes
- lettuce to serve
- hot sauce to serve
- wraps to serve
- mayo to serve

## Method
1. Prepare three coating stations, i.e. large bowls or deep dishes
2. In the first dish, mix the flour, paprika, and salt
3. In the second dish, gently scramble the egg
4. In the third dish, mix the panko, mixed herbs, and chilli flakes
5. Cut the chicken into bite-sized pieces and add to the first dish, thoroughly mixing until every piece is evenly coated and dry. Add more paprika or flour if needed.
6. Transfer the now dry chicken pieces to the egg bowl, and again coat evenly
7. Preheat the oven to 180C (fan)
8. Prepare a baking tray, and one-by-one transfer a piece of egged chicken into the breadcrumbs, use one hand for this, and the other to coat the chicken in breadcrumbs and transfer to the baking tray.
9. Cook the chicken for 25 minutes, or until cooked through and crispy
10. Serve alongside lettuce, hot sauce, and wraps, so people can construct their own wraps
