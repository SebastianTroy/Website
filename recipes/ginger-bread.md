## Description
The perfect blend of crisp and gooey gingerbread, makes 25 gingerbread men. Roll thivker for more goo, and thinner for more crisp.

## Ingredients
- 125g stork dairy free spread block
- 100g dark muscuvado sugar
- 4 tbsp golden syrup
- 325g plain flour
- 1 tsp bicarbonate of soda
- 4 tsp ground ginger

## Method
1. Melt the spread, sugar, and syrup in a pan, stirrin occasionally, then remove from the heat.
2. Sieve all powders into a mixing bowl and stir in the melted ingredients, until you have a firm dough.
3. Pre-heat the oven to 170C (fan)
4. Roll out to ~0.5cm on a floured surface, if using cookie cutters, dip them in flour before each cut to prevent sticking.
5. Place the cookies on baking trays lined with baking paper.
6. Cook for ~10 minutes, until lightly browned.
7. Cool on a rack, wait until completely cool if decorating.
